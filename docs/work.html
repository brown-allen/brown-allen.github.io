OSU Ecampus Learning Analytics Cohort

One of 12 participating researchers in the Perceptions of Learning Data in Higher Education study. This interview-based study collected information about student, faculty, staff, and administrator views of how data is used to understand the student experience or to improve student learning in higher education. The project was designed with a systems perspective in mind in order to explore benefits and challenges of the use of learning and learner data, ethics and privacy issues and concerns among different stakeholder groups, and ideas and perceptions regarding the current and future uses of learning and learner data.
